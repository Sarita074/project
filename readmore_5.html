About this item

UV Protection: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can cause fading and cracking of the dashboard. This polish includes powerful UV protection, forming a protective barrier that shields the dashboard from harmful sun rays, preventing premature wear and maintaining its original color.

Restores Shine: Over time, a car's dashboard can lose its original shine and appear dull. Our Dashboard Polish revitalizes the surface, restoring a glossy finish, and enhancing the richness of the dashboard's materials.

Versatile Application: The Car Dashboard Polish is suitable for a wide range of dashboard materials, including leather, vinyl, plastic, and rubber. It can also be used on door panels and center consoles, offering a comprehensive solution for your car's interior care.

Easy Application: Applying our polish is a breeze. Allowing you to achieve even coverage without any hassle.
